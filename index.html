Sun

The Sun is a nearly perfect sphere of hot plasma and the most important source of energy for life on Earth. It is about 4.6 billion years old and comprises approximately 99.86% of the solar system's total mass.

Earth-River-Lives

Earth is the third planet from the Sun, uniquely harboring life with diverse ecosystems and climates. Rivers, as lifelines of the planet, support various forms of life by providing essential water resources and sustaining rich biodiversity across their courses.

Moon

"The moon, Earth's natural satellite, orbits our planet, influencing tides and serving as a celestial beacon in the night sky."
